👋 Meet the Team!

Hi, we are Oscar (8) and his sibling! Oscar is saving up his money and absolutely loves dogs. We are starting Oscar's Paws & Claws to help local neighbors give their pups some extra exercise.

Because we are in school, we are available on Weekends, Bank Holidays, and School Holidays. We always go on the walks together as a team to make sure every dog stays safe and happy!
